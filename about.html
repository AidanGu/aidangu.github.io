About Me

Building Community Through Code

From Cambridge classrooms to Santa Cruz maker spaces, I'm passionate about creating technology that brings people together and empowers communities to learn and grow.

Hello, I'm Aidan

I'm a Computer Engineering student at UC Santa Cruz with a passion for creating technology that brings people together. My journey spans from working as a Maker Space Intern through the CUIP program to conducting research at the Tech4Good Lab.

Beyond coding, I'm deeply involved in community building and creative technology. As an Adobe Student Brand Ambassador, I help others learn creative tools and techniques that empower their projects and ideas.

When I'm not working on projects or planning events, you'll find me playing music, exploring new technologies, or helping others build confidence in tech.
